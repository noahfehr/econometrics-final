SEC. 9218. REPORT ON CHANGES TO THE FOREIGN SERVICE OFFICER TEST.

Not later than December 1, 2023, the Secretary shall submit a report to the appropriate congressional committees describing and justifying any changes made during fiscal years 2022 and 2023 to the Foreign Service entry process, including--
	(1) the use of artificial intelligence, including deep textual analysis, in any portion of the entry process and its impacts on recruitment into the Foreign Service;
	(2) the use of virtual formats for any portion of the entry process and its impacts on recruitment into the Foreign Service; and
	(3) the entities, groups, or individuals informed of or consulted on any changes to the Foreign Service entry process during the 1-year period immediately preceding the implementation of such changes.
